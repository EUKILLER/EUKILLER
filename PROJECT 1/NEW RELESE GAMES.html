NEW RELESE GAMES

  1. AEW Fight Forever

    2. AEW Fight Forever is a professional wrestling sports video game developed by Yuke's and published by THQ Nordic. It is the debut title on home consoles and personal computers from American professional wrestling promotion All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

  2. Aliens: Dark Descent

    3. Aliens: Dark Descent is a real-time strategy video game developed by Tindalos Interactive in collaboration with Disney's 20th Century Games and published by Focus Entertainment. Set in the Alien universe, the game was released for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S on June 20, 2023. It received generally positive reviews from critics.

  3. F1 23

    4. F1 23 is a racing video game developed by Codemasters and published by EA Sports. It is the sixteenth entry in the F1 series by Codemasters. It holds the official licence for the 2023 Formula One and Formula 2 championships. The game was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, and Linux (through Valve's Proton compatibility layer) on 16 June 2023.[a]
